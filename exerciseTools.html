Best Home Exercise Equipment & Machines

Don’t you have time, energy or money to go to the gym? Here you will find information about the best home exercise equipment that do not cost a lot of money, but they can help you to train your entire body, develop your cardio and endurance. I listed those tools that I have in my home gym, so I know they are useful. I use them almost every day and I’m really happy that I have bought them. I can work out whenever I want. I do not have to wait for anybody, so my workouts more efficient and continuous. You can invest into more or just one to start with. No matter which you choose, you will get home workout equipment that will help perform efficient exercises to lose weight, build muscle and improve your cardiovascular system.

1. Jump Rope

There is a reason why boxers use the jumping rope so much. This simple fitness equipment is one of the best ways for cardio. Jumping helps to burn calories, improves your cardiovascular system and strengthens almost all of your muscles. It is cheap, and you can use and take it anywhere.
I do jump roping regularly since I started boxing, and it has a positive impact on my endurance and speed. I can punch the heavy bag longer than ever before.
It is a cheap home gym equipment. The basic models cost only some bucks. The weighted ones cost a little bit more, but you can maximize the speed with the help of them making your workouts even more efficient.
The only thing you need to keep in mind when your want to purchase one is to have enough space to use it comfortably.

2. Punching Bags

Boxing is unquestionably the best cardio workout that trains the entire body, boost the heart rate up and helps to burn fat. It is also one of the most efficient practices to release stress and develop your endurance.
There are two kinds of punching bags you can buy. Smaller and lighter ones (speed bag) which help to improve the accuracy of punches, and heavy bags for harder punches and for kicking.

Since your aim is not being a professional fighter, I recommend you the heavy bag. heavy punching bag for home

I perform heavy bag training 2-3 times a week for 45-50 minutes. I'm sure that this sort of training, besides my diet, is the main reason I lost over 35 pounds in less than half a year.

Furthermore, my fitness level is better than ever before. I studied the punch types by watching the videos on Youtube where I also found many routines. After learning the basic punches and some simpler combinations, I have increased the time slowly. Now I can hit the heavy bag for 10×3 minutes. ​



A punching bag is maybe not the cheapest exercise equipment for home, and you may need other accessories such as gloves, but it is worth every penny. It is a complete indoor cardio workout. ​

Before buying one do not forget that you will need enough space to use it, and you will have to install it.

3. Dumbells

Dumbbells are essential home gym workout equipment since they can be used many ways. You can train all the major muscle groups with them.

Dumbbells are necessary for strength training and isolating muscles. Moreover, they help to improve your coordination and balance since dumbbells exercises need more control. They are the best buy exercise equipment for both men and women to get stronger and build muscles.

We can purchase dumbbell sets that include many different weight pieces. We can also get adjustable dumbbells such as Bowflex SelectTech that are the most comfortable solution but a bit more expensive. dumbbells

Keep in mind that if you buy separate weights you will need quite a lot of space to store them or you will need to purchase a rack. That is why adjustable ones are better since they combine 10-20 sets of weights.

4. Kettlebells

Kettlebells have similar benefits as dumbbells, but since they have a unique shape, they are maybe more efficient. Plus we can perform unusual exercises with them. A lot of people think it is the best small home gym equipment.

They are very popular these days recommended by the qualified fitness trainers. There are a lot of kettlebell workouts for full-body training.

You can buy kettlebells in various sizes, and there are even adjustable ones.

5. Stability Ball

A fitness ball is especially useful for working abs, but it can be used for upper and lower body workouts, as well. It is also beneficial fitness equipment for rehabilitation, to perform stretching exercises or even to sit on it that helps to improve your posture.

Most of the activities done by using an exercise ball improve the posture and work several muscles at once since we have to keep the balance.

With the help of it, you can better standard moves like push-ups. It is also the best at home exercise equipment for seniors at home.

6. Chin Up Bars

If you do not have enough money or space for a body tower, a chin-up bar is the solution for you. Chin up bars can be fixed by using the frame of the door, or there are mounted versions. It is the best exercise equipment for small apartment for bodyweight training.

With the help of it, you can do different pull ups, chin ups and many abdominal exercises such as hanging leg raises.

There are cheap ones with no extra functions, but the better ones do not cost so much as well.

7. TRX Suspension Trainer

TRX is a rather new system that lets us perform a lot of types of bodyweight exercises. This commercial fitness equipment has been developed by the Navy Seals, and the famous fitness trainers recommend it.

TRX system is extremely beneficial as it combines the stability and strengths motions. For instance, it is particularly useful for core training, you can check out some examples here. You can strengthen and tone each muscles groups with the help of this portable exercise equipment.

8. Resistance Bands

After watching a video how boxers use a resistance band to improve their punching power, I purchased one.

There are various types from simple, cheaper ones to more complex that offer almost the some benefits as TRX.

By using this lightweight fitness equipment, you can enhance traditional exercises since you can add extra resistance to movements. There are so many ways you can use it.

9. Mats

A good stretching mat is essential if you want to do yoga or other workouts on the floor. They do not cost so much but help to perform the movements more comfortably and safely. For instance, it is useful for doing ab workouts on the floor.

10. Adjustable Sit Up Bench

A sit up bench is still a powerful tool to train abs at home, and an adjustable one is even better as we can modify the angle in many ways. This way we can increase or decrease the resistance. Also, the most professional products lets you to use it as a weight bench.

11. Roman Chair

A Roman chair or so called hyperextension bench is the best home gym equipment to train lower back and to avoid of lower back pain. Besides, we can use it for abdominal exercises, for dips and other moves. There are some models that can be used as an ab bench, too.

12. Ab Wheels

The ab wheel is the best compact home exercise equipment to strengthen your abs. It is an extremely efficient tool to work core muscles. Besides it trains back, arms, chest, and shoulders.

13. Medicine Balls

Medicine balls seem to be too simple equipment, but they are very useful to enhance various exercises, mainly for the upper body. Medicine balls come in different sizes and weight, so you can surely find the one that fits your fitness level. They are suitable for both cardio and strength training.

14. Treadmill

There is a reason why boxers use the jumping rope so much. This simple fitness equipment is one of the best ways for cardio. Jumping helps to burn calories, improves your cardiovascular system and strengthens almost all of your muscles. It is cheap, and you can use and take it anywhere.
I do jump roping regularly since I started boxing, and it has a positive impact on my endurance and speed. I can punch the heavy bag longer than ever before.
It is a cheap home gym equipment. The basic models cost only some bucks. The weighted ones cost a little bit more, but you can maximize the speed with the help of them making your workouts even more efficient.
The only thing you need to keep in mind when your want to purchase one is to have enough space to use it comfortably.

15. Exercise Bike

If you do not like running then, an exercise bike is a solution for home cardio workouts. It is small footprint exercise equipment that is great to have a low impact cardio workout. Plus, it works the lower body efficiently as well.

The basic models are affordable and perfect for any average user. Although, the more expensive ones provide far more functions. It is maybe the most suitable at home workout equipment for small rooms.

16. Foam roller

The foam roller is a small exercise equipment that has a lot of health benefits. Training with it increases the range of motion, decrease muscle soreness and pain, increase muscle length and flexibility. It is a great way to prevent workout injuries or helps rehabilitation.

Conclusion

As you see, there are a lot of types of gear and exercise machines for home you can purchase. I have almost all the mentioned products, and I use them. Of course, you can buy a compact home gym or other cardio equipment such as an elliptical trainer, but you can have good workouts at home with the mention equipment above.

However, none of these items will help you to get stronger, slimmer or lose weight if you do not use them. Do not buy anything until you determine that you are going to change your body.
